## Assault & Battery

<img src="images/assaultbattery.png?raw=true"/>

**Project description:** You’re a lone battery, trying to keep that spark alive, but others have different ideas. Fight your way through an endless battle between you and the robots seeking to take your charge! Evade their attacks and dash right through the armor  to take their charge for yourself Upgrade your abilities from the scrap you find to face the increasingly hostile electronic world. How long can you last?

Created in 48 hours for the University of Michigan WolverineSoft Shammy 2024 Game Jam. I contributed the cover image, tilemap, various attacks, and environmental elements.

Play it on [Itch.io](https://randomgamestuff.itch.io/assault-and-battery)!
