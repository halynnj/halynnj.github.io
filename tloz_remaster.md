## The Legend of Zelda Remaster

<img src="images/dummy_thumbnail.jpg?raw=true"/>

**Project description:** A Unity remaster of the first dungeon from the Legend of Zelda on NES. The aim of the project was to faithfully recreate the first dungeon of TLOZ and its mechanics, including player and enemy movement, player attacks, health and damage, and level design. I also created a new light-based mechanic; press 4 to enter the custom level!

Play it on [Itch.io](https://hellojelloo.itch.io/the-legend-of-zelda)!
